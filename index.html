About Me

I spent most of my adult life in graduate school studying Anthropology. I had every intention of obtaining my Ph.D. and pursuing a career teaching Anthropology at a major univeristy. After six years, I found myself unsatisfied and growing increasingly frustrated with academia. I chose to leave and vowed to really take my time finding a new path about which I could be truly passionate.

This is how I found my way to the Coding Boot Camp. I am very excited to start this new chapter of my life.
